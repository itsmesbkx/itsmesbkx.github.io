A little background

I'm Konstantinos Xeiletzaris and I was born in Athens, a capital and largest city of Greece. Since I was young, I was capturing memories. I was shooting photos and videos, from various places, my friends, my loved ones and generally everything around me. Later in my life something sparked inside me. So when I was in high school, photography and videography became a hobby. My first photos where averege and my videos where shaky. Day by day, my skills and my experience got improved. Now I'm enough experienced, to start doing photography and videography as career. However my skills doesn't stop improving. Everyday I keep practicing, finding mistakes and correct them. And now with other artists that I met and with academic studies, I will grow even more, not only on my career, but also as a person.

My goal is to meet new people, make dreams come true through photography & videography and do my work as skillful as I can.

Currently, for academic reasons, I can work part-time or freelace only on weekends.
